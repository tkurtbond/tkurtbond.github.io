1.1. Setting

This campaign was set in a heavily modified version of The Frontiers of Alusia, a very minimal setting (with a rather nice map) published by SPI[2] for their DragonQuest roleplaying game.


[2] Simulations Publications, Inc., an influential and prolific game publisher of the 1970s and 1980s.
