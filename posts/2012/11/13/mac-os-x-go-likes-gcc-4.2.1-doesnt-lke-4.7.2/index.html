Mac OS X go likes gcc 4.2.1 doesn't lke 4.7.2

T. Kurt Bond

2012-11-13 13:58

Source

I have gcc from MacPorts (“gcc version 4.7.2 (MacPorts gcc47 4.7.2_2)”) installed on my laptop running Mac OS X 10.6.8, and when updated and recompiled go the tests failed in the race detector with a message about a MachO segment being missing. I put /usr/bin/gcc at the front of the path, and everything compiled tested fine.
