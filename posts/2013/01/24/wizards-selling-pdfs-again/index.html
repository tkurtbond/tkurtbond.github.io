Wizards selling PDFs again

T. Kurt Bond

2013-01-24 12:08

Source

I’m glad that Wizards of the Coast is selling PDFs of their old D&D products again. The fact that they've made it possible for folks who bought their previous PDFs from rpgnow.com/drivethrurpg.com to redownload them (and apparently in some cases at least, new, improved versions of the PDFs) is a major plus to me. And having Shannon Appelcline write historical notes on many of the products is a great plus.
