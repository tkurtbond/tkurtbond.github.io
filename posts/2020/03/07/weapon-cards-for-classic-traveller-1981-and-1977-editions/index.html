Weapon Cards for Classic Traveller (1981 and 1977 editions)

T. Kurt Bond

2020-03-07 19:08

Source

Chris Kubasik of Tales to Astound has two sets of Weapon Cards for Classic Traveller (for the 1981 and 1977 editions) that simplifies Classic Traveller combat by consolidating the the information needed for each weapon.

There is also a page with a PDF of pre-generated characters and a blank character sheet.
