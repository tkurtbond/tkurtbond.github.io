Runequest Reborn?

T. Kurt Bond

2005-10-16 00:00

Source

Well, it looks like Mongoose is serious about their new version of Runequest. It's also apparently going to be an “Open” system, so that publishers and individuals will be able to able to produce new derived works using the Runequest rule. Moreover, it's going to get its own treatment of Glorantha, too. I sure hope this turns out well, but I also hope it doesn't prove a problem for HeroQuest.
